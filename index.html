Drawing in My Life By: Abby Robinson

Drawing is one of the many hobbies that I have enjoyed all my life. My artistic talent came from both my parents. My mother is artistically gifted, and my father is musically gifted. I have been drawing since I was a baby, but I never took it seriously until I was a junior in highschool and I finally took a legitimate art class.

Type of Art Example of Drawing
Cubism-a movement in art, esp. of the early 20th cent., characterized by a breakdown of the subject into cubes and other geometric forms in abstract arrangements rather than by a realistic representation
Surrealism-unnerving, illogical scenes with photographic precision, created strange creatures from everyday objects, and developed painting techniques that allowed the unconscious to express itself
